Ascension Public School Since 2013

Ascension Public School was established on 5th June, 2013 under the guidance of the then Archbishop, Most Rev. Bernard Moras. It was an initiative by the then Parish priest, Rev. Fr. Deva Kumar. He felt the need of education to the children of poor and backward classes of Maruthi Seva Nagar. It was basically to empower poor and the marginalised people through education.

This institution has been working tirelessly to uplift the poor children with proper education regardless of their caste, religion or creed. In the beginning, there were only three teachers and 26 children but today it has grown to be a big esteemed institution. Today, the school gives the best of holistic formation with regard to academics, sports and extracurricular. The school also imparts good values and takes the formation of the future citizens of the country as their mission and vision.

Ascension Public school is an English medium school and it strictly encourages one community and one language ideology in the school campus. The staff is well trained and the facilities are at their best.

Today the school looks at training the students not only for a career but also for their future personal lives. Hence, it is indeed a best school for those looking for a well-trained human being.
